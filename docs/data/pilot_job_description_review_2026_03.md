# Pilot Job Description Review

Review batch: `pilot_2026_03`

This document records the first reviewed job-description pass for five calibration occupations:
- Lawyers
- Data Scientists
- Management Analysts
- Technical Writers
- Sales Representatives

Source registry for the reviewed postings lives in `data/metadata/job_description_review_sources.csv`.

## Lawyers

Reviewed postings:
- Affirm - Senior Counsel, Ethics & Employment
- Epic Games - Senior Product Counsel, Children's Privacy & Design
- Fullscript - Senior Counsel, Commercial, Data and Strategic Partnerships
- Instawork - Senior Commercial Counsel
- Alchemy - Associate General Counsel

Promoted task additions:
- translate changing regulations into practical launch and operating guidance
- build playbooks, templates, policies, and training that scale compliant execution
- lead investigations and high-risk legal escalations with coordinated stakeholders
- structure strategic partnerships and data arrangements under legal constraints

Rejected as duplicates:
- generic contract negotiation
- generic legal research and precedent analysis
- generic courtroom or agency representation
