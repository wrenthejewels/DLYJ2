Understanding your output

Your v2.1 result is a structured role briefing, not a prediction with certainty. It estimates where AI pressure lands first, what that pressure spreads into, and how much of the role still holds together afterward.

The result combines the occupation task inventory, reviewed task additions, dependency links, retained-function signals, empirical automation priors, and your optional role-refinement answers. Visit the Methodology for the pipeline and the Guide for how to read the briefing.
