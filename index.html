Understanding your output

Your v2.1 result is an empirical role briefing, not a certainty. It ranks your task clusters by automation difficulty into three waves (current, next, distant) and estimates whether the remaining bundle holds together as a coherent role.

The main result is driven by the occupation task inventory, dependency links between tasks, retained-function signals, empirical automation priors, and your role-refinement answers. Visit the Methodology for the pipeline and the Guide for how to interpret the briefing.
